Lion

Lion is known to be the King of Beasts ("king of the jungle" would be a misnomer) across most cultures of the world.

  • Scientific name: Panthera leo
  • Average length: 2.1 meters
  • Average lifespan: 12 years
  • Diet: Zebras, goats, nilgai, buffaloes

The lion (Panthera leo) is a species in the family Felidae and a member of the genus Panthera. It is the second largest extant species after the tiger. It exhibits a pronounced sexual dimorphism; males have prominent manes and are larger than females. The lion's mane is the most recognisable feature of the species.
